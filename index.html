How this site works

34thward.com collects public updates from elected officials, local businesses, civic organizations, religious organizations, and community newsletters that serve the 34th Ward. New items are gathered automatically each day from email newsletters and public feeds, summarized, and published here.

Items that name a specific person in connection with an allegation or dispute are held for a brief human review before publishing. Everything else publishes automatically. Every item links back to its original source.

This site aggregates and summarizes third-party content. It is not the official site of any elected official, and inclusion of an organization does not imply endorsement.
